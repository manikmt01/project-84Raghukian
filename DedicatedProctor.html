DEDICATED PROCTOR

NthEye offers dedicated proctoring, a higher level of customization and control compared to live and auto proctoring solutions. It includes "pure white labeling" where the client can use their own logo and information, and control data and servers used during the proctoring process. This is ideal for large organizations with a high number of test-takers and strict data and security regulations. NthEye's specialized proctoring system offers a thorough and adaptable proctoring service that ensures the institution's brand is accurately represented and regulations are followed. Dedicated proctoring offers additional control and customization, making it a great choice for institutions in need of a proctoring solution.

The benefits of using our dedicated proctoring service

Flexibility

Test takers can begin exams at any time and have the freedom to schedule them according to their availability thanks to NthEye's 24/7 proctoring service. To guarantee that the exam is administered fairly and securely, the service confirms the test-taker's identification and continuously monitors the testing environment. To preserve the integrity of the exam, the system also records any ambiguous situations that might occur throughout the process and addresses them. Test takers and institutions can be confident that the exam procedure is fair and secure thanks to NthEye's proctoring service.

Exam supervision

NthEye provides thorough and safe supervision for online tests and quizzes. Highly trained proctors who are equipped with cutting-edge technology and AI-based monitoring to find suspicious events deliver the service. Exam sessions are taped beginning to end, giving administrators and proctors a complete view of the session for later review. The tool helps schools and students ensure the security and fairness of online exams by providing live look-in capabilities as well as the reporting of questionable conduct. NthEye is dedicated to offering a safe and impartial online exam environment.

Trusted service provider in the sector

In order to guarantee the security and fairness of online exams, NthEye places a high priority on customer satisfaction and has a staff of knowledgeable information security professionals. The team is committed to giving consumers the finest service possible and is aware about the most recent trends, technology, and laws in the sector. They are receptive to customer needs and prepared to go above and beyond to guarantee client pleasure. In the proctoring and information security industries, NthEye is regarded as one of the most reliable and reputable service providers.

Detailed Reports

The proctoring service from NthEye provides documented proof and real-time notifications of any confirmed suspicious activity during a test. Proctors have 24-48 hours to act after the exam and can access the screen recording and evidence with speed and efficiency. Proctors and administrators can better assess the system's functioning and pinpoint areas for improvement thanks to the system's flagging and time-stamping features.

Affordable
