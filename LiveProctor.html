Live Proctor

NthEye's live proctoring solution ensures the integrity of online tests at every stage. A live proctor monitors the fairness and security of the exam and intervenes, if necessary, while AI-based monitoring and reporting capture any suspicious conduct. The service also protects exam integrity by recording the suspicious activities (webcam recording and screen videos) while monitoring mouse and keystroke behaviour. Full protection, reporting, and on-demand recording are also provided. NthEye's live proctoring offers a secure and comprehensive method for conducting online exams, giving both institutions and students peace of mind about the fairness and security of the exam process.
