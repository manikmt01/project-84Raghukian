Services

To meet the needs of many industries, we provide a broad range of services.

Auto Proctor

Employing cameras, microphones, and sensors, auto proctoring involves using AI to watch test-takers take online examinations. This monitoring helps to catch cheating and other questionable conduct, which is subsequently assessed by a human proctor.

Learn More

Live Proctor

Through a live video feed, a human proctor keeps an eye on test-takers taking online tests when live proctoring is used. The proctor authenticates the student's identification, checks that the exam's regulations are being followed, and alerts them to any questionable activity. This strategy provides greater protection and a more individualized strategy.

Learn More

Dedicated Proctor

To ensure exam integrity, dedicated proctoring assigns a human proctor (available 24/7) to a particular student or group of test-takers during an online exam. The proctor keeps an eye on the student's webcam and microphone and could request proof of identification. When cheating or improper behavior is suspected during high-stakes or high-security exams, it is employed.

Learn More

Features

Screen sharing and screenshots are not permitted

In order to prevent proxy cheating, our online proctoring solution offers a function that conceals the exam screen during screen sharing and capture. The proctor can keep an eye on the student's screen, including keystrokes and mouse movements, to look for any suspicious behavior, preserving the integrity of the exam and ensuring fair evaluation of each student's performance.
