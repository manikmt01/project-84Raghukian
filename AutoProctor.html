AUTO PROCTOR

NthEye provides a secure and fair solution for online exams through its fully automated platform with video capture, multi-factor identification verification and AI-based behavior tracking. The system records students' activity and detects any cheating or misconduct with AI algorithms. Proctors can monitor exams in real-time and review saved screenshots, audio and video data for any violations. To prevent cheating, the proctor can view the student's screen and hide the exam screen. The platform also prohibits screen sharing and exam capture to maintain exam neutrality. NthEye offers a complete and efficient solution for online test proctoring.
