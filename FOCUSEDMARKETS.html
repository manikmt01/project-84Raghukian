Proctoring solutions are needed in many
different markets, including:

01

Education

Online proctoring is essential to verify the validity of online tests and assessments in education. It monitors students remotely during exams to prevent academic dishonesty and cheating. The benefits of online proctoring include increased integrity of exams, more flexible timing and accessibility, compliance with accreditation standards, and useful performance information. Its significance is expected to persist due to the pandemic and rise of remote learning.

02

Exams for competitive certification in the workplace

Online proctoring is crucial in maintaining the validity of professional certifications and competitive exams. By using technology to remotely monitor candidates, cheating is prevented, and test integrity is maintained. The advantages of online proctoring include more flexible timing and accessibility, compliance with industry standards, and useful performance information. The significance of online proctoring is expected to persist in the future with the ongoing pandemic and increasing remote work.

03

Hiring and recruiting

Online proctoring is crucial in the hiring and recruitment process. It uses technology to remotely monitor job candidates during tests or interviews to prevent cheating or receiving help. Key benefits include maintaining process integrity, allowing for flexible timing and accessibility, meeting industry standards, and providing useful performance data. Its significance is expected to persist due to the pandemic and remote work.

04

Government and armed forces

Online proctoring is essential for government and military exams to maintain test integrity and ensure candidate suitability. It prevents cheating by allowing remote proctoring and is flexible in terms of timing and accessibility. Online proctoring also ensures compliance with rules and best practices and provides insights on candidate performance.

05

IT and cybersecurity

Online proctoring is vital for upholding the legitimacy of certifications in cybersecurity and IT, avoiding cheating, offering flexible scheduling and accessibility, following industry standards, and providing data on candidate performance. It aids in ensuring that only trained and skilled personnel work in these crucial industries, protecting both people and businesses.

06

Services and financial institutions

Online proctoring is crucial for financial institutions and businesses as it helps preserve certification validity and ensures personnel are equipped for their jobs. Online proctoring prevents cheating, offers flexible scheduling and accessibility, enforces rules and best practices, and provides information on candidate performance. Online proctoring ensures only qualified individuals work in critical fields that require expertise and knowledge to secure financial transactions and protect the financial system.

07

Healthcare

Online proctoring is increasingly important in the healthcare sector as online learning and certifications grow in use. It ensures the validity of online exams and assessments for healthcare workers to keep their licenses and certifications, especially as telemedicine and remote healthcare increase. Proctoring offers security and oversight, enables flexible exam-taking locations, and helps prevent cheating by only allowing certified individuals to work in healthcare.
